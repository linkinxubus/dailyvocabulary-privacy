Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Vocabit ("we", "our", "the app") is a vocabulary learning app. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

Data We Collect

Vocabit does not collect personal information. The app does not require an account, login, or registration. All your data — saved words, learning progress, preferences, and settings — is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • AppLovin MAX (advertising) — Displays ads to free users. AppLovin may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization, subject to your App Tracking Transparency choice. AppLovin Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat (in-app purchases) — Processes subscriptions and purchases. RevenueCat receives anonymous transaction data from Apple. RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Advertising & Tracking

The app shows ads to non-premium users via AppLovin MAX. Before any ad-related tracking occurs, the app asks for your consent through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt. If you decline, ads are still shown but are not personalized. Premium subscribers see no ads.

Children's Privacy

Vocabit is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. We do not operate servers or databases that store your information. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
